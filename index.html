About Me

My name is Tejasva Bangar. I was born in Rohtak on 10 April 2006. I completed my Class 10 from Faridabad and Class 12 from Delhi, and currently I am pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at IIIT Sonepat. Although I have studied in different cities, my hometown is Rohtak, which always keeps me connected to my roots.

Rohtak, my hometown, has a rich history and culture, but like many places in India, it also faces challenges. In present times, migrant communities, including people from Bangladesh, live in Rohtak, contributing to the workforce but also facing hardships such as lack of proper housing, limited access to education, and employment difficulties. Additionally, local people of Rohtak face problems like increasing traffic, pollution, and unemployment, which affect daily life.

Despite these challenges, I strongly believe in the potential of technology, innovation, and youth to bring positive changes to society and improve lives in places like Rohtak and beyond.
